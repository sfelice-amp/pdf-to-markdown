Work Breakdown Structure (WBS) 3 Facility The Woodsmith Facilities have been broken down into a series of four-digit facility codes per the table below. Only those Facility Codes with 'YES' marked in the 'TAGS' column shall be used in the allocation of tag numbers. This additional guidance has been included to ensure all items within a facility are consistently and unambiguously tagged using a single facility code, recognizing that certain codes are intended only for capture of indirect costs or for roll-up reporting. For those facility codes used for tagging, the Level 1 (L1) and Level 2 (L2) codes represent facilities within a defined plant area / geographical location. The Level 3 (L3) and Level 4 (L4) codes represent a further grouping of the facilities under their respective L2 plant area by function and/or logical breakdown of the works.
